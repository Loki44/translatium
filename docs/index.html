Saying goodbye to Translatium

In 2014, Translatium was created to fill the need of a Google Translate app for Windows Phone. Since then, the app's expanded to support Windows PC and macOS. And thousands of users used the app daily.

But nothing lasts forever. Translatium relies on an unofficial Google Translate API. And as the API becomes more and more unreliable recently, it's no longer feasible to keep the app working properly. In light of that, we’ve made the difficult decision to sunset Translatium.

As of November 7th, 2018, Translatium was removed from Microsoft Store and Mac App Store. If you've purchased Translatium, please contact Microsoft or Apple to ask for a refund. If you need any further information, email us at support@translatiumapp.com.

Thank you for using Translatium.
